What is Instanssi?

Instanssi is a festival of digital making in Jyväskylä, Finland. It is organized 9.-11.3.2011 at Voionmaan lukio. Instanssi is meant to be a beginner friendly event, which everyone who is interested in digital culture can visit and enjoy. Instanssi offers workshops, presentations and friendly creative environment. This year we concentrate especially on open data. Unfortunately most of the page is currently only available in Finnish.

Competitions at Instanssi include demo, 4k demo, music, graphic, fast graphic, scraping of open data, Robocode and random, in which the point is to make a confusing entry any way you like. One of the specialties of Instanssi are the programmable lights, which can be used as a part of the entries.

You can get more info on our IRC channel at #instanssi (on IRCnet).

The address of the venue is Voionmaankatu 17-19, 40700 Jyväskylä, Finland. Check the map right here.

Tickets

Tickets can be bought from the door or beforehand from the Internet. From the door you can also get a ticket, which gives you one time entrance and costs 2 Euros

Dynamic ticket – 5 Euros. This ticket includes entrance to the event for the whole weekend. You can watch all the program, participate in the workshops and use the showers and WLAN. There is also sleeping space available.

Static ticket – 10 Euros. This ticket includes everything that the Dynamic ticket offers, plus network, electricity and table space (90x70cm) for your computer. Note: static tickets have been sold out.
