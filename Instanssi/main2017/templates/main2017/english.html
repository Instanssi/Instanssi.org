What is Instanssi?

Instanssi is a festival of digital creativity in Jyväskylä, Finland. Instanssi is meant to be a beginner friendly event that anyone who is interested in digital culture can visit and enjoy. Instanssi offers workshops, presentations and a friendly creative environment. Unfortunately most of the website is currently only available in Finnish.

Competitions at Instanssi include classics such as demo, 4k demo, music, graphics, gamedev and random, which is meant to confuse the audience using any means necessary. One of the specialities of Instanssi are programmable lights, which can be used as a part of the compo entry.

You can ask questions and get more info on our IRC channel at #instanssi (on IRCnet).
