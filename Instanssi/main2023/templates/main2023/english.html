What is Instanssi Revision Satellite?

Instanssi is a festival of digital creativity in Jyväskylä, Finland. Instanssi is a beginner-friendly event that anyone interested in digital culture can visit and enjoy. Instanssi offers workshops, presentations, and a friendly creative environment.

This year, Revision will get the on-location help from organizers all around the world and Instanssi is one of them. In addition to Revision's online stream, there will be Satellite events which enables you to still have drinks and chats with your friends without having to resort to sofascening all weekend.

In addition to Jyväskylä, there are events planned all over Europe. See the full listing.

Although Instanssi is a Revision Satellite event, we still want to keep the warm atmosphere of Instanssi. We'll have those DMX lights for you to play with! And free coffee, of course.

Date and venue

Instanssi Revision Satellite event will be organized at Easter, from Friday 15th to Monday 18th April 2022.

Tickets

Tickets can be bought from the door or beforehand from the Instanssi store on our website.

  • Entrance ticket (kävijälippu) – 10 Euros. This ticket includes entrance to the event for the whole weekend. You can enjoy the program, participate in the workshops, and stay the night.
  • Early Bird Computer place ticket (konepaikkalippu) – 30 Euros. This ticket allows entrance to Instanssi and a table spot for your computer. Sold until the end of March.
  • Computer place ticket (konepaikkalippu) – 40 Euros. This ticket allows entrance to Instanssi and a table spot for your computer.
  • Support ticket (ökylippu) – 50 Euros. For those who want to support Instanssi! This ticket allows entrance to Instanssi and a table spot for your computer. You can also say a few words at the opening ceremony if you like.

What do I need to take with me?

Bring the same necessities as you would to any other LAN-party or demoparty. Here is a small checklist to help you.

  • If you have a ticket with a computer place, bring your computer with all the necessary accessories. Bring a long power cord and your own extension cord. Remember to write your name on your stuff!
  • A sleeping bag and pad
  • Change of clothes
  • Personal toiletries: towel, toothbrush etc. There is a shower on the venue.
  • Cash if you are buying your tickets from the door
  • Your own mug for free coffee and tea
  • Snacks and non-alcoholic beverages. There is a microwave oven, a coffee maker, and a kettle for free use on the venue. Fridge space is limited, so bring your own cooler (Peltier coolers allowed).
  • Demoparty vibes!

What NOT to bring

A list of forbidden items

  • Prescription drugs without a prescription slip and ID
  • Alcohol
  • Narcotics
  • Firearms, edge tools, and other possibly harming objects.
  • Multifunctional tools
  • Fireworks and other explosives
  • Laser pointers and similar objects
  • Own domestic appliances (Fridges, coffee makers, washing machines etc.)
